Privacy Online

What are the concerns of privacy online?

It is important to know the dangers of privacy online. For example, surprisingly many people are not aware they do not own the data that they input online. They are not aware that it is collected, sold to third parties, and used for targeted advertising. It matters that people should be educated about this, so those not aware can learn. Many people just click “I agree” the terms and service on a website, without reading them and knowing the full consequences. The everyday user is not aware what terms like “targeted advertising and “data mining” mean. That is what this website is there to fix. The goal is to educate people, so they are more aware online.

What is "Data mining" and "targeted advertising"?

Data mining is the technique of gathering large amounts of information on someone, then typically, using this information along with machine learning, to create a pattern, to make a prediction. This prediction comes into play with “Targeted advertising”. Targeted advertising is when a company, or a website uses the prediction they made using machine learning to show ads that a user will most likely click on. There are four main ways ways of doing this. This website does not get into them, but you can find more information here.
